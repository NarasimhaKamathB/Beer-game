⏳ Each upstream player receives the order one week later.
By the time Manufacturer knows, the demand is already old news.

📈 A small demand shift becomes a huge swing upstream.

This is the Bullwhip Effect — the reason the Beer Game was invented.

👤 Customer
Demand changes from 4 → 8
🛍️ Retailer
Panics slightly — orders extra buffer
🏪 Wholesaler
Even more uncertainty — over-orders
📦 Distributor
Huge swing — 4× demand volatility

🎯 Your job: resist the urge to over-order and keep the chain stable.

💰 Every decision has a price.

Whether you hold too much or too little, you pay. Your goal is to minimise total team cost.
